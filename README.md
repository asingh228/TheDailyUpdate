### ⚡ 2025-12-08: Enhancing Retrieval-Augmented Generation with Entity Linking for Educational Platforms
> **Simple Summary:** Here's an explanation of the research paper in simple terms:

The paper describes a new way to improve the accuracy of AI-powered question-answering systems, specifically designed for educational settings. This system combines two techniques: one that uses semantic similarity (like understanding the meaning of words) and another that uses entity linking (like connecting words to specific facts or objects in a database). By combining these two, the system can provide more accurate and reliable answers to questions.

This research matters because it can lead to the development of more effective AI-based tutoring tools that can help students learn better and more accurately. By improving the accuracy of these systems, educators can rely on them more confidently to support student learning and achieve better outcomes.
