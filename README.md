

### ⚡ 2025-12-08: Enhancing Retrieval-Augmented Generation with Entity Linking for Educational Platforms
> **Simple Summary:** Here's an explanation of the research paper in simple terms:

**What it does:** This AI research aims to improve the accuracy of question-answering systems used in educational settings, such as online tutoring platforms, by combining two approaches: one that looks for similar information online and another that looks for specific, accurate facts.

**Why it matters:** By using both approaches together, the system can provide more reliable and accurate answers to educational questions, which is especially important in specialized fields where small mistakes can have big consequences. This advancement has the potential to create more effective, AI-based tutoring tools that can help students learn and understand complex concepts more reliably and efficiently.
